Patient Intake

This example shows how a patient can provide SMS consent before receiving scheduling links, appointment reminders, confirmations, or callback messages.

Messages may include imaging exam scheduling links, appointment reminders, confirmations, callback coordination, and HELP/STOP responses from Advanced Medical Imaging. Message frequency varies, up to 6 messages per imaging order. Consent is not a condition of receiving medical care. See our Terms and Privacy Policy.
